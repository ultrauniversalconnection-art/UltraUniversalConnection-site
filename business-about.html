Why we built LYNQFAST

Most of us on the LYNQFAST team have worked as engineers, IT leaders, or remote workers ourselves. We know the pain of buffering calls, unexplained outages, and support tickets that vanish into a black hole.

So we started from a different premise: build the network we wished we had – and back it with support we would actually want to call.

Predictable performance

We engineer our capacity and routes for modern usage: streaming, cloud apps, video conferencing, and large uploads – not just outdated speed tests.

Clear, honest pricing

No intro rates that triple in the second year. No surprise fees hidden behind long invoices. What you agree to is what you see.

Real human support

When something goes wrong, you should not have to explain your entire setup from scratch every time. Our teams are trained to own issues end‑to‑end.

Values that guide how we operate
