Our Story

Founded in 2009 with a simple mission: to make sure consumers never have to struggle alone with confusing electronics. What started as a small local support team has grown into a trusted independent service, but we've never forgotten our roots.

We began by recognizing that shoppers deserved better after the checkout line — clear guidance, honest advice, and hands-on help setting up the products they'd bought. Our technicians are trained across the leading TV, streaming, and smart home brands so we can help no matter what you own.

Today, we're proud to have helped over 50,000 customers nationwide, but our commitment remains the same: independent, unbiased support with a personal touch that only a customer-focused company can provide.
