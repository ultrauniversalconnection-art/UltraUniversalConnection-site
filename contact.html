Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Are you affiliated with Walmart or the retailer I bought my product from?

No. We are an independent service provider and are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or authorized by Walmart Inc. or any other retailer. We simply help consumers set up and troubleshoot products they've purchased.

Does it matter where I bought my TV or streaming device?

No. Our services are available regardless of where the product was purchased or which brand it is.

How quickly can you help me?

Call us and we'll start with remote troubleshooting right away, or schedule an on-site visit for setup and installation at a time that works for you.

Which devices can you help with?

We support Smart TVs, Roku, Fire TV, Apple TV, Chromecast, and most other streaming and home entertainment devices, regardless of brand.

Can you help me choose a TV or streaming device before I buy?

Yes. Because we're independent, our product buying guidance is unbiased and focused on what fits your needs and budget.

What if I have an ongoing issue after setup?

Our support team is available to help you troubleshoot and resolve any problems that come up after your initial setup or installation.

Need Urgent Support?

If you're experiencing a TV, streaming device, or connectivity issue, our support team is available to help existing customers.
