Quick self‑checks before you call

Try these steps to resolve the most common home internet issues. If you still have trouble, we are ready to help.

1. Power‑cycle your equipment

Unplug your modem or ONT and router for 30 seconds, then plug them back in and wait 2–3 minutes.

2. Check cabling & lights

Ensure all cables are firmly connected and look for unusual light patterns on your modem or ONT.

3. Try a wired test

Connect a laptop directly via Ethernet and run a speed test to compare Wi‑Fi vs. line performance.
